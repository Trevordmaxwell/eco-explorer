# Signature Pocket And Threshold Destination Phase

## Summary

The next traversal wave should keep spending scope on memorable authored places.

Forest already has benchmark destination energy. The next strongest move is to give:

- coastal scrub one unforgettable signature pocket
- the high country one clearer threshold destination that players remember by feel

## Goals

- turn coastal scrub into a more emotionally distinct place through one strong traversal pocket
- deepen treeline or tundra threshold identity through one memorable physical beat
- keep all new spaces cozy, readable, and recoverable

## Wave Shape

### Step 1

- prepare the coastal scrub signature-pocket traversal handoff

### Step 2

- implement one coast-side signature pocket or lee-space destination

### Step 3

- review whether that destination now gives coastal scrub benchmark place memory

### Step 4

- prepare the high-country threshold destination handoff

### Step 5

- implement one compact threshold destination in treeline or tundra

### Step 6

- review whether the high country now has a more unforgettable shelter or exposure beat

## Guardrails

- do not add punishing traversal or larger maze structure
- avoid widening the world map or route shell
- make the new places feel authored and tactile, not procedural
