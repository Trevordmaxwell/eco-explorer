# Regional Travel And Season-Two Approach Phase

## Summary

This is a future lane-1 follow-on after directional travel coherence.

Once corridor entries, exits, and map returns feel directionally believable, the next small systems polish step should help the player feel how the world opens outward into the next season. The goal is not another large route board or map UI. The goal is one calmer regional approach layer:

- map-return posts
- region-facing approach cues
- one tiny next-district invitation

## Why Now

Travel coherence fixes confusion. This follow-on should add warmth and intention after that fix. It gives the player:

- a better sense of where a longer outing begins
- a cleaner return rhythm
- one small “what next” pull after the current season closes

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare one compact regional travel and map-return post pack.
2. Implement that pack without opening a new navigation surface.
3. Review the result.
4. Prepare one calm second-season district invitation.
5. Implement that invitation.
6. Review whether the station and map now point forward more gracefully.

## Guardrails

- Do not replace the hybrid map-plus-corridor model.
- Do not add a new map HUD or planner.
- Prefer a few strong spatial cues over extra text.
- Keep this inside lane 1.

## Intended Result

After this phase, travel should feel both clearer and more inviting: the player should better understand where they are returning from and what outward direction the next season is gently asking them to take.
