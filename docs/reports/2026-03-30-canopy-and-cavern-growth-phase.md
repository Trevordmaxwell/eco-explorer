# Canopy And Cavern Growth Phase

## Summary

Lane 3 already has the right first shape: runtime vertical budget, a first giant-tree pocket, a first deeper cave, and a small bridge between them. The next wave should not jump to a huge cave engine or a giant open-world canopy. It should grow the lane one careful step in each direction:

- higher into the canopy
- deeper into cave chambers
- clearer through tiny wayfinding and recovery cues
- more coherent through one optional crossover beat

## Why Now

The lane needs a second growth wave so the vertical family feels durable rather than like a one-off experiment. The best way to do that is with authored extensions, not a rewrite.

This wave should prove that lane 3 can support:

- taller spaces that still read well
- deeper spaces that still feel cozy
- optional routes that reward curiosity without precision-platform punishment

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare an upper-canopy pocket extension.
2. Implement the upper-canopy extension.
3. Review canopy readability and traversal feel.
4. Prepare a multi-chamber cave continuation.
5. Implement the cave continuation.
6. Review cave readability and atmosphere.
7. Prepare tiny wayfinding and recovery cues for tall and deep spaces.
8. Implement those cues.
9. Review whether the cues help without turning into a HUD layer.
10. Add one optional canopy-to-cavern crossover beat.
11. Review the full expanded vertical-exploration family.

## Guardrails

- Do not add combat, fail-state danger, or survival framing.
- Do not build a generalized cave-topology engine in this phase.
- Keep giant trees grounded in Pacific old-growth cues and scale cheats rather than literal species simulation.
- Keep all traversal friendly and readable at the current handheld scale.
- Prefer authored pockets and chambers over broad procedural expansion.

## Intended Result

After this wave, lane 3 should leave the game with:

- a more convincing upper canopy
- a more layered cave family
- clearer navigation in tall and deep spaces
- a stronger sense that vertical exploration is a real long-term pillar of the game
