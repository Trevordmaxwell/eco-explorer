# Home-Place And Feedback Phase

## Summary

The next lane-1 wave should make the station and returns feel more tangible.

The recent external review points to a clear product need:

- the structure around outings is stronger than the physical payoff of returning
- the station and nursery understand the player's progress, but they do not yet show it strongly enough
- future depth at `256x160` should come more from visible feedback than from more words

## Goals

- make station and nursery progress more visibly earned
- strengthen departure, arrival, and route-complete feedback without adding another UI shell
- keep the station as a calm home place, not a dashboard

## Wave Shape

### Step 1

- prepare one visible home-place evolution pass

### Step 2

- implement one compact visual evolution layer at the station or nursery

### Step 3

- review whether the home place now feels more shaped by the player

### Step 4

- prepare one non-text feedback pass for departure, arrival, or route completion

### Step 5

- implement one compact motion or feedback layer using existing seams

### Step 6

- review whether the new feedback improves feel without clutter

## Guardrails

- do not add another station page
- do not widen the board or atlas copy budget
- prefer visual, motion, and small environmental payoffs
- keep the home place warm and compact
