# Nursery Reflection And Copy-Budget Phase

## Summary

Renderer cleanup alone will not fix the nursery if the page is still trying to say too much at once.

Lane 2 should make the authored side cleaner by:

- hard-capping nursery copy by state
- separating plant state, ecological reflection, and utility language into cleaner authored beats
- keeping the home loop science-forward without turning the card into a paragraph stack

## Goals

- define clear per-state copy ceilings for nursery cards
- keep ecological reflection concise and distinct from reward or utility language
- preserve calm, readable teaching on the smallest home-loop surface

## Wave Shape

### Step 1

- prepare a nursery copy-budget and state-beat handoff

### Step 2

- implement authored per-state nursery copy caps and reflection splits

### Step 3

- review whether the authored nursery copy now respects the handheld budget

### Step 4

- prepare one reward-label or naming-normalization follow-on

### Step 5

- implement one compact nursery naming or reward-copy cleanup

### Step 6

- review whether the nursery now teaches clearly without crowding the card

## Guardrails

- do not widen route-board or station copy
- do not add more nursery facts just because space exists
- prefer one compact sentence per beat over stacked explanation
