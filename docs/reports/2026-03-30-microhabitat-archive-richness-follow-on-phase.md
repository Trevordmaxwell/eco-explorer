# Microhabitat Archive Richness Follow-On Phase

## Summary

This is a future lane-2 follow-on after the current content-richness wave.

The next content-only growth step should deepen the newly emerging microhabitats around old-growth, under-root, and cave-adjacent spaces, then give them one quiet archive or sketchbook payoff inside existing surfaces.

## Why Now

Lane 3 is making the world taller and deeper. Lane 2 should eventually make those new spaces feel inhabited and memorable without reopening route logic or traversal runtime.

This phase is about:

- science-safe discoveries
- note-backed context
- comparison-ready anchors
- one compact archive-facing payoff

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare a microhabitat content pack.
2. Implement that pack.
3. Review the new content.
4. Prepare one archive or sketchbook payoff for those microhabitats.
5. Implement the payoff.
6. Review whether the new spaces now feel memorably collected instead of merely visited.

## Guardrails

- Do not widen route or station logic.
- Keep the archive/sketchbook payoff inside existing surfaces.
- Prefer narrow, well-grounded packs over broad taxonomy sprawl.
- Keep the lane content-owned.

## Intended Result

After this phase, the newer old-growth and cave-adjacent spaces should feel denser, more teachable, and more worth remembering without needing any new shell or system.
