# Canopy Wayfinding And Cavern Loop Phase

## Summary

This is a future lane-3 follow-on after the current canopy-and-cavern growth wave.

The next vertical-exploration step should make those spaces easier to read and more satisfying to revisit by adding:

- one upper-canopy continuation
- one calmer cavern-loop follow-up
- one tiny layer of wayfinding and recovery support

## Why Now

Lane 3 is already building real height and depth. The next risk is not lack of space. It is whether those spaces stay readable, welcoming, and memorable at handheld scale.

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare one upper-canopy continuation.
2. Implement it.
3. Review readability and route feel.
4. Prepare one cavern-loop and wayfinding follow-up.
5. Implement it.
6. Review whether the vertical family now feels more coherent end to end.

## Guardrails

- Do not add combat, danger meters, or a cave maze.
- Keep wayfinding tiny and in-world instead of HUD-heavy.
- Preserve kid-friendly traversal and forgiving recovery.
- Keep the work inside lane 3.

## Intended Result

After this phase, the giant-tree and deep-cave family should feel like a more complete exploration branch with clearer route logic, better return rhythm, and stronger place identity.
