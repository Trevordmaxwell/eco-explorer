# Vertical Cooldown And Regression Phase

## Summary

The latest review explicitly advises against another beach or treeline destination push right now. Those spaces are near their comfortable handheld-density ceiling.

Lane 3 should spend one wave on protection instead of expansion:

- clean up any small spacing or recovery seams in the current benchmark spaces
- add stronger traversal guardrails and regression coverage
- prepare later vertical growth without shipping more geography this round

## Goals

- preserve readability in the newest beach, treeline, bluff, and tundra spaces
- harden current traversal spaces with cleanup and tests instead of more landmarks
- keep lane 3 productive without pushing density higher

## Wave Shape

### Step 2

### Step 3

- review whether the cleanup protects feel without expanding scope

### Step 4

### Step 5

## Guardrails

- prefer spacing, recoverability, and regression protection over new content
- keep all changes small and behavior-preserving
