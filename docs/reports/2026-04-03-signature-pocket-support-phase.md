# Signature Pocket Support Phase

## Summary

Lane 2 should support the game's most memorable places instead of just adding more totals.

The review highlights:

- `Forest` already feels like the benchmark biome
- `Coastal Scrub` still risks reading as connective tissue instead of a destination
- `Treeline` and `Tundra` should lean harder into exposure, threshold, and seasonal harshness as gameplay texture

## Goals

- give `Coastal Scrub` stronger notebook, comparison, and close-look identity
- give `Treeline` and `Tundra` a sharper exposure-threshold support pack
- keep this all inside existing content-facing seams

## Guardrails

- no world-map, route-board, or station logic
- no broad ledger-only work with no player-facing payoff
- prefer place memory and ecological relationship over raw item count
