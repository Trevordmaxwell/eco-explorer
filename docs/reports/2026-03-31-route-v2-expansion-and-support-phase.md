# Route V2 Expansion And Support Phase

## Summary

Lane 4's next wave should move beyond the current forest chapter and bring more of the outing loop up to the new standard.

The next best Route v2 growth is:

1. add one stronger non-forest outing type that feels more like a transect or process read than a hidden checklist
2. add one more calm pre-outing support and a slightly richer notebook filing payoff

This keeps the game-loop lane focused on choice, interpretation, and closure instead of opening a bigger quest shell.

## Why Now

The current Route v2 core, support slot, and `ROOT HOLLOW` chapter prove the shape works. The next gain is to spread that feeling into one more route family and strengthen the return payoff a little.

Doing that now is higher leverage than inventing another broad system.

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare the first post-forest Route v2 expansion pack.
2. Implement one stronger new outing type and convert one route family to use it.
3. Review whether the route now feels more like a purposeful outing than a compact checklist.
4. Prepare one calm support-slot and notebook-return follow-on.
5. Implement that support and filing-payoff pass.
6. Review whether the support choice and notebook finish feel more satisfying without adding clutter.

## Guardrails

- Do not add a new station page, quest log, or inventory shell.
- Keep support growth tiny and optional.
- Reuse existing world-state and filing seams.
- Keep the tone cozy, notebook-first, and science-forward.

## Intended Result

After this phase, more than one route family should feel like a real outing with a small choice and a satisfying notebook return, not just `ROOT HOLLOW`.
