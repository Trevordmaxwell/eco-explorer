4. Prepare the first treeline lee-side ascent proof.
5. Implement the treeline ascent proof.

## Guardrails

- Keep the spaces cozy and recoverable.
- Reuse the current tiny cue language.

## Intended Result

After this phase, lane 3 should own one forest vertical destination space and one promising treeline vertical proof, giving future expeditions more biome variety.
