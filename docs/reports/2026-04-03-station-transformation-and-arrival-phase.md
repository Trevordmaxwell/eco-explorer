# Station Transformation And Arrival Phase

## Summary

After the visible home-place evolution and the first controller split, lane 1 should keep pushing tangible payoff instead of more shell density.

This follow-on wave should:

- deepen visible station transformation across continued play
- improve arrival and departure feel through compact non-text seams

## Why Now

The review's strongest lane-1 call was that the home loop understands progress more than it shows it.

This phase keeps solving that problem by:

- making the station feel more shaped by the player's season
- giving travel and returns stronger punctuation without adding new panels

## Guardrails

- no new station page or dashboard
- no extra board text density
- prefer visible changes, small motion, and environmental punctuation
- keep the station warm, compact, and kid-readable
