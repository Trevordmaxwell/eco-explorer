# Atlas And Sketchbook Richness Phase

## Summary

Lane 2's later long-push wave should deepen the player's memory and authorship payoff through the atlas and sketchbook without opening a bigger archive shell.

The next best gains are:

1. one compact atlas-facing richness pass
2. one sketchbook follow-on that makes a few remembered routes or habitats feel more authored

## Why Now

Once the richer content waves have landed, the next content-facing payoff is not more systems, but a stronger sense that the player's remembered world is layered and personal.

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare the atlas-facing richness handoff.
2. Implement the atlas-facing pass through existing filed-memory or note seams.
3. Review whether the remembered world now feels deeper.
4. Prepare one sketchbook-richness follow-on.
5. Implement the follow-on inside the existing sketchbook shell.
6. Review whether the authorship payoff still stays light.

## Guardrails

- Do not add a larger archive page.
- Do not add a freeform sketch editor.
- Reuse the current atlas, note, close-look, and sketchbook seams only.

## Intended Result

After this phase, the atlas and sketchbook should feel richer and more personal without becoming a second game.
