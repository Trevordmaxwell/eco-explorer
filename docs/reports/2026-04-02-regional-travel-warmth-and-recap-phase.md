# Regional Travel Warmth And Recap Phase

## Summary

Lane 1's next systems wave should keep season two feeling alive outside the core outing itself.

The next best gains are:

1. stronger regional travel warmth in the inland half of the map
2. a calmer return recap seam that helps the player remember what just changed without opening a bigger planner surface

## Why Now

The game now has cleaner season-two launching and route discoverability, but the travel and return rhythm still has room to feel more authored. The player should sense that they are moving deeper inland and then coming back with a little more orientation than before.

## Recommended Order

1. Prepare an inland regional-travel warmth handoff.
2. Implement the travel-warmth pass through existing map, post, and footer seams.
3. Review whether travel now feels more grounded and less generic.
4. Prepare one compact return-recap follow-on.
5. Implement the return-recap pass inside existing station and map surfaces.
6. Review whether the recap gives closure without growing the shell.

## Guardrails

- Do not add a new top-level station page.
- Do not add a larger travel HUD.
- Reuse the current map footer, map-return posts, routes strip, and expedition seams.
- Keep the recap tiny and optional.

## Intended Result

After this phase, travel should feel more regionally grounded and returning from an outing should feel a little more intentionally closed.
