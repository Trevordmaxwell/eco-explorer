## Product Aim

The next phase should make the game feel like it is moving from a proven beta chapter into a broader second-field-season runway:

- the station and map should be calmer about what comes next
- the world should feel denser, especially across coast, forest, and high country
- vertical and corridor spaces should become more memorable without new traversal frameworks
- route play should feel more authored through support, replay, and filing depth
- proof should stay strong enough that future agents need less hand holding

## Anti-Drift Rules

- No season-three shell, biome six, combat, crafting, inventory, broad economy, direct API mode, or larger planner.
- No fourth Source to Shore beat unless a scout packet explicitly proves station, route, map, journal, and save capacity first.
- Lane 2 does not edit station or route shells.
- Lane 3 does not edit station, route-board, or route catalog semantics.
- Lane 4 does not absorb content packs or geometry work.
- Lane 1 does not absorb content, spatial, or route-authoring work except final integration proof.
