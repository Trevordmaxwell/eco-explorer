## Status policy

For a standard four-lane packet:

- four scouts start `READY` if the packet is active;
- four mains start `BLOCKED` on their scouts;
- four critics start `BLOCKED-BY-IMPLEMENTATION` on their mains.

Only the first active packet should have `READY` scouts. Later packets should stay `BLOCKED` or be represented as packet seeds until the director activates them.
